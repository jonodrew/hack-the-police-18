Submitting video evidence to the police

Thank you for agreeing to submit your video. If you can edit your video down to the most valuable parts, please do so. However, if you don't have the software or don't feel comfortable, please submit the entire video. Once you've done this you'll receive an email confirmation with a code that refers specifically to your video, if you gave us an email, or a text if you gave us your phone number.
